Lasagna Recipe

Description

This mouthwatering lasagna recipe is a classic Italian dish that is sure to impress. Layers of tender pasta, rich meat sauce, creamy cheese, and aromatic herbs come together to create a delightful culinary experience. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or simply craving some comfort food, this lasagna recipe is a perfect choice. It's hearty, flavorful, and guaranteed to leave your taste buds wanting more.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt and pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the lasagna noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. In a skillet, brown the ground beef with chopped onions and garlic.
  4. Add tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper to the skillet. Simmer for 10 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, mix ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, and salt.
  6. Assemble the lasagna by layering noodles, meat sauce, ricotta cheese mixture, and mozzarella cheese in a baking dish.
  7. Repeat the layers until all ingredients are used, ending with a layer of cheese on top.
  8. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25 minutes.
  9. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  10. Let the lasagna cool for a few minutes, then serve and enjoy!
